Your Journey Starts Here

The soothing sound of water, fine spray soaring in the air and privacy. The emotions that waterfalls give are difficult to compare with what you experience when visiting other locations. Agree, the very word "waterfall" excites the imagination. It is possible to surprise friends and relatives by inviting them to “drive them to the waterfall” in Belarus - hardly anyone can resist. All the available river level drops in our country are man-made, but there are a few places that are definitely worth a visit. Among them, the waterfall near the village of Prudniki in the Miory region stands out.

Today, such a landmark of Belarus as Miory waterfalls is known to many tourists and is a wonderful place for relaxation. Many have understood that a vacation in the Vitebsk region is not only an opportunity to wander through dense forests, breathe in crystal clear air, but also a way to feel like a tourist in an expensive resort, plunging into the abyss of the powerful jets of this waterfall. By the way, around the waterfall you will find a well-equipped area with gazebos, benches, tables. Here you can swim in still water and sunbathe. And somewhere in the background, the rumbling of a waterfall will be heard.
