Our Story

Belarus is a country with an ancient and rich history, a distinctive culture. The greatest value for the development of educational tourism are: historical and cultural monuments - the historical center of Grodno, the monuments of Polotsk, the architectural and cultural complex of the former residence of the Radziwills in Nesvizh, the Mir castle complex, the Brest fortress, the Lida castle,

That is why we want to introduce guests to our country and its culture and develop tourism in Belarus.
